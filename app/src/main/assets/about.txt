<h2>About PoLA (Pocket Lab App)</h2>

<b>Version:</b> 0.1<br>
<b>Developed by:</b> <i>Lukasz Kaczmarek, PhD., Adam Mickiewicz University, AMU Psychophysiology Laboratory: Gaming & Streaming</i><br>
<b>Release Date:</b> <i>March 14th, 2024</i><br>

<hr>

<b>Overview:</b><br>
<p>PoLA (Pocket Lab App) is a mobile application designed to advance the way behavioral research is conducted. Developed with the needs of researchers and participants in mind, PoLA offers a versatile platform for conducting a wide range of studies, from simple surveys to complex experimental protocols, in real-time and naturalistic settings. Leveraging the integration with PoLA's AI-powered <a href="https://chat.openai.com/g/g-Vz0JnWtqf-pola-helpful-assistant-hela">HELPFUL ASSISTANT (HelA)</a>, it streamlines the process of experimental research development.</p>

<b>Key Features:</b>
<ul>
    <li><b>Dynamic Study Protocols:</b> Easily upload and manage custom study protocols. Create and revise protocols effortlessly using natural language instructions with HelA.</li>
    <li><b>Real-time Data Collection:</b> Securely collect and store responses on the device, maintaining data integrity and confidentiality.</li>
    <li><b>Alarm Management:</b> Schedule notifications and reminders to keep participants engaged and ensure timely task completion.</li>
    <li><b>User-friendly Interface:</b> Minimum instruction is needed for participants to navigate through studies, enhanced by tutorials and demo protocols.</li>
    <li><b>Offline Functionality:</b> Enables studies to be conducted without an internet connection, in any location.</li>
       <li>
         <b>AI-Powered Protocol Creation:</b> HelA assists in the generation and revision of experimental protocols using natural language processing. Redirect to <a href="https://chat.openai.com/g/g-Vz0JnWtqf-pola-helpful-assistant-hela">HELPFUL ASSISTANT</a>.
       </li>
</ul>

<p><b>Getting Started:</b> Upon installation, explore the app through running demo and tutorial protocols. Utilize HelA for protocol creation and revision by providing natural language instructions.</p>
